## EMOTIONAL UNDERTONES TO DETECT

| Undertone | Trigger Phrases | Response Mode |
|-----------|-----------------|---------------|
| Worry | "Should I be concerned", "Is this normal" | Reassurance first |
| Guilt | "I feel bad", "Did I do wrong" | Acknowledge, no judgment |
| Overwhelm | "I don't know where to start", "Too many options" | Simplify, 2-3 choices |
| Grief | "Lost", "Passed away", "Not ready" | COMFORT mode |
| Excitement | "Can't wait", "So excited" | Match energy, celebrate |
