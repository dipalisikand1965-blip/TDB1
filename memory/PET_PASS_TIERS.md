# Pet Pass Membership Tiers

## Tier System (Member Journey)

### 1. Curious Pup 🐕 (New Member)
- **Description**: New members starting their Pet Pass journey
- **Criteria**: Just activated Pet Pass (Trial or Foundation)
- **Duration**: First 30 days OR until first service used
- **Points Multiplier**: 1x

### 2. Loyal Companion 🦮 (Active Member)
- **Description**: Members who have engaged with at least 2 pillars
- **Criteria**: 
  - Used 2+ different pillars
  - OR 3+ months active membership
  - OR ₹5,000+ lifetime spend
- **Points Multiplier**: 1.5x

### 3. Trusted Guardian 🛡️ (Established Member)
- **Description**: Core members with deep engagement
- **Criteria**:
  - Used 5+ different pillars
  - OR 6+ months active membership
  - OR ₹15,000+ lifetime spend
  - OR Referred 2+ members
- **Points Multiplier**: 2x
- **Benefits**: Priority concierge, early access

### 4. Pack Leader 👑 (Elite Member)
- **Description**: Most engaged and loyal members
- **Criteria**:
  - Used 8+ different pillars
  - OR 12+ months active membership
  - OR ₹50,000+ lifetime spend
  - OR Referred 5+ members
- **Points Multiplier**: 3x
- **Benefits**: VIP concierge, exclusive events, partner perks

## Auto-Graduation Rules

Members automatically graduate to next tier when criteria are met.
No manual action required. System checks daily.

## Never Downgrade
Members never lose their tier, even during renewal gaps.
Once earned, always earned.
