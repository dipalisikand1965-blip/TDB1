# MIRA OS - THE 14 PILLARS BIBLE
## Definitive Reference for All Agents
## Status: INDICATIVE (Keep Adding To This)

---

> **A pillar represents how a pet LIVES across its lifetime.**
> Not services. Not commerce. Not features.
> Each pillar = a life domain.

---

## 🏛️ THE 14 PILLARS

### 1️⃣ CARE
**Physical wellbeing maintenance and preventive health.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Grooming routines
- Skin & coat hygiene
- Dental hygiene
- Parasite prevention
- Wellness routines
- Routine health monitoring
- Hygiene maintenance

❌ **Does NOT include:**
- Diagnosis
- Medication advice
- Veterinary treatment decisions

---

### 2️⃣ DINE
**Nutrition, feeding behaviour, and food relationship.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Diet structure
- Meal planning
- Feeding routines
- Food sensitivities
- Hydration
- Treats & supplements
- Transitions

❌ **Does NOT include:**
- Shopping as a category
- Medical therapeutic diet prescriptions

---

### 3️⃣ STAY
**Where the pet lives and rests.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Boarding
- Pet sitting
- Home setup
- Comfort environments
- Sleep arrangements
- Safe spaces

> This is about **habitat security**.

---

### 4️⃣ TRAVEL
**Movement across locations.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Transport logistics
- Travel preparation
- Documentation
- Carriers
- Mobility planning
- Destination readiness

> This is about **safe relocation**.

---

### 5️⃣ ENJOY
**Play, stimulation, emotional joy.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Enrichment
- Toys
- Play environments
- Social play
- Novelty experiences
- Mental stimulation

> This is about **happiness activation**.

---

### 6️⃣ FIT
**Physical movement and body function.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Exercise planning
- Mobility maintenance
- Physical conditioning
- Recovery movement
- Activity rhythm

> This is about **physical vitality**.

---

### 7️⃣ LEARN
**Behaviour development and structured knowledge.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Training
- Skill building
- Behaviour shaping
- Education guides
- Learning frameworks
- Knowledge resources
- **YouTube videos (curated, as supporting content only)**

> Media (videos, articles) = supporting only, never the destination.

---

### 8️⃣ CELEBRATE
**Emotional milestones and memory creation.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Birthdays
- Adoption anniversaries
- Rituals
- Photography
- Events
- Memory keepsakes

> This is about **meaningful moments**.

---

### 9️⃣ ADOPT
**Life entry and family integration.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Adoption readiness
- Breeder / rescue evaluation
- Temperament matching
- Transition into home
- Integration planning

> This is about **life beginning with the family**.

---

### 🔟 ADVISORY
**Professional guidance coordination.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Second opinions
- Nutrition consults
- Behaviour consults
- Specialist coordination
- Expert reviews

> This is about **informed decision support**.

---

### 1️⃣1️⃣ PAPERWORK
**Identity, compliance, and legal records.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Vaccination records
- Licensing
- Travel documents
- Microchip registration
- Certifications

> This is about **official existence**.

---

### 1️⃣2️⃣ EMERGENCY
**Acute risk and immediate response.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Urgent care routing
- Emergency transport
- First-response guidance
- Crisis coordination

> This is about **survival protection**.

---

### 1️⃣3️⃣ FAREWELL
**End-of-life transition and memory preservation.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Hospice coordination
- Memorial services
- Remembrance rituals
- Grief support

> This is about **life closure with dignity**.

---

### 1️⃣4️⃣ SERVICES
**Execution and fulfilment layer.**

⚠️ **This is NOT a life domain. This is how action happens.**

✅ **Includes:**
- Bookings
- Sourcing
- Scheduling
- Subscriptions
- Task management
- Coordination
- Fulfilment tracking

> Everything operational lives here.

---

## 🧭 HOW TO THINK ABOUT THIS STRUCTURE

```
Life Domains (pet existence)
→ Pillars 1 to 13

Execution Mechanism
→ Pillar 14 (Services)
```

---

## ❗ WHAT IS NOT A PILLAR (CRITICAL)

These are NOT pillars because they are mechanisms or tools:

| NOT a Pillar | Lives Inside |
|--------------|--------------|
| ❌ Grooming | CARE |
| ❌ Shop / Shopping | SERVICES |
| ❌ Pet Photography | CELEBRATE |
| ❌ Products | Respective pillars |
| ❌ Videos / Content | LEARN |
| ❌ Marketplace | SERVICES |
| ❌ Bookings | SERVICES |
| ❌ Orders | SERVICES |
| ❌ Subscriptions | SERVICES |

---

## 🧠 PILLAR TEST (RULE FOR ALL AGENTS)

To qualify as a pillar, it must answer:

> **"Is this a fundamental dimension of a pet's lived existence?"**

**If no → it is not a pillar.**
