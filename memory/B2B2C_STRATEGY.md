## 💡 THE PIVOT

### Current Model Problems:
- B2C customer acquisition = ₹500-2000 per customer
- High marketing spend, low retention
- Competing with Amazon, Supertails, Heads Up For Tails
- The Doggy Bakery already handles B2C cakes/treats

### New Model Solution:
**Don't acquire customers. Acquire PARTNERS who already have customers.**

## 💰 REVENUE MODEL COMPARISON

### B2C (Current - Expensive)
```
Customer Acquisition Cost: ₹500-2000
Average Order Value: ₹1,500
Gross Margin: 30%
Payback Period: 3-5 orders
```

### B2B2C (Proposed - Efficient)
```
Partner Acquisition Cost: ₹10,000-50,000
Partner brings: 100-10,000 customers
Effective CAC: ₹5-50 per customer
Revenue per partner: ₹50,000-5,00,000/year
```

### Revenue Streams

| Stream | B2C | B2B2C |
|--------|-----|-------|
| Product Sales | ✅ High effort | ✅ Partner sells |
| SaaS Subscription | ❌ | ✅ ₹2,999-50,000/mo |
| Transaction Fee | 0% | 2-5% |
| API Access | ❌ | ✅ Usage-based |
| Data Licensing | ❌ | ✅ Premium |
| White-label | ❌ | ✅ Setup + monthly |
