# THE DOGGY COMPANY - FOUNDATIONAL DOCTRINE

## The North Star
> **"The longer a pet lives with us, the less their parent has to explain."**

---

## 1. FIRST PRINCIPLES

### The Core Truth
- There is **NO such thing as a "member" without a pet**
- Member = Pet Parent (Hooman)
- Membership eligibility exists **only if at least one pet is registered**
- No browsing, no checkout, no concierge access without a pet profile

### The Hierarchy
```
Pet Parent Account (Hooman) → One or More Pet Souls
```

The **Hooman exists to serve the Pet**. Not the other way around.

---

## 2. CORE OBJECTS

### A. Pet Parent (Hooman Folder)
Replaces generic "Member". Contains:
- Personal details (name, contact, city)
- Membership status & tenure
- Payment history
- Communication preferences
- Linked Pets (1-to-many relationship)
- Conversation history (Mira AI, WhatsApp, support)
- Orders, services, shipping, subscriptions

### B. Pet Soul
The **heart of the system**. This is:
- A **living intelligence object**
- Built over time
- Continuously enriched
- Read and written to by **every system touchpoint**

> **This is not a form.**
> **This is not onboarding.**
> **This is an evolving digital soul.**

---

## 3. THE 8 SOUL PILLARS (Locked In)

| # | Pillar | Icon | Purpose |
|---|--------|------|---------|
| 1 | Identity & Temperament | 🎭 | Who they are at their core |
| 2 | Family & Pack | 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 | Their social world |
| 3 | Rhythm & Routine | ⏰ | Daily patterns |
| 4 | Home Comforts | 🏠 | What makes them feel safe |
| 5 | Travel Style | ✈️ | How they explore |
| 6 | Taste & Treat | 🍖 | Food personality |
| 7 | Training & Behaviour | 🎓 | How they learn |
| 8 | Long Horizon | 🌅 | Dreams and hopes |

Each pillar:
- Has weighted questions
- Has a completion score
- Can be partially known
- **Improves through inference, not just answers**

> **Important: Not all knowledge comes from questions.**

---

## 4. HOW THE PET SOUL GETS SMARTER

### The Four Data Entry Points (ONLY THESE)

#### 1. Direct Answers
- Soul questions (in-app, WhatsApp, Mira AI)

#### 2. Behavioral Inference
- What they **buy**
- What they **repeat**
- What they **avoid**
- What they **return**

#### 3. Service Interaction
- Grooming bookings
- Travel requests
- Celebrations
- Care services

#### 4. Conversation Intelligence (Mira AI)
- Tone
- Preferences
- Sensitivities
- Frequency
- Emotional cues

### The Golden Rule
Every interaction must ask:
> **"Does this tell us something new about the Pet Soul?"**

**If yes → write to Pet Soul.**
**If no → do not ask again.**

---

## 5. MIRA AI'S ROLE

### What Mira Is
> **Mira is not a chatbot.**
> **Mira is the recognition and orchestration layer.**

### Mira Must:
1. **Always recognise the Pet by name** once known
2. **Never re-ask known information**
3. Ask **one relevant question** at a time
4. Use existing Pet Soul context to **infer defaults**

### Example - The Right Way:
✅ *"Noted. I already have Mojo as a medium, long-coat dog. I'll proceed accordingly."*

### Example - The Wrong Way:
❌ *"Please confirm size and coat type."*

> **This is a trust system.**

---

## 6. PROGRESSIVE SOUL BUILDING

### Weekly Soul Drip via WhatsApp

**The Formula:**
- **One question at a time**
- **Once a week**
- **Contextual** (never random)
- **Auto-mapped** to the correct Soul Pillar
- **Auto-updates** Pet Soul on reply

### Examples:
- *"Does Mojo get anxious when left alone for more than 3 hours?"*
- *"What's Mojo's favourite reward after a walk?"*
- *"How does Mojo usually react to new dogs?"*

**No links. No forms. Just conversation.**

### What This Achieves:
1. Builds Soul richness **without fatigue**
2. Trains the system to **feel human**

### Avoid:

> **This is a premium operating system.**

## 8. PET VAULT PHILOSOPHY

### Positioning
> *"A personal care memory, not medical advice."*

### The Vault:
- **Stores** facts
- **Triggers** reminders
- **Enables** continuity
- **Never** diagnoses

> **This protects trust and liability.**

---

## 9. INTELLIGENT COMMERCE

### As Pet Soul Deepens:
- Product recommendations become **narrower**
- Autoship becomes **predictive**
- Messaging becomes **less frequent but more accurate**
- Offers feel **earned, not pushed**

### Example - Respect in Action:
A dog with:
- Grain sensitivity
- Senior age
- Low energy

Should **NEVER** see:
- Puppy treats
- High-protein training snacks
- Random festival offers

> **This is respect.**

---

## 10. NON-NEGOTIABLES

1. **Pet Parent (Hooman)** is the account
2. **Pet Soul** is a first-class object
3. **Every system** must read/write Pet Soul
4. **Mira AI** must use Pet Soul context live
5. **Questions are progressive**, never repetitive
6. **Recognition > Recommendation**
7. **Silence is better than irrelevant messaging**

---

## 11. SYSTEM BEHAVIOR CHECKLIST

### Before Any Interaction, Ask:
- [ ] Do I know which Pet this is about?
- [ ] Have I checked the Pet Soul first?
- [ ] Am I about to ask something I already know?
- [ ] Will this interaction teach me something new?
- [ ] Is this message relevant to THIS pet specifically?
- [ ] Can I infer the answer instead of asking?

### After Any Interaction, Ask:
- [ ] Did I learn something new about the Pet Soul?
- [ ] Did I update the Pet Soul with this learning?
- [ ] Did the Pet Parent have to repeat themselves?
- [ ] Did I acknowledge what I already knew?
