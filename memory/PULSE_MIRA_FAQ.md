## 🤔 Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Pulse?
**Pulse ⚡** is your quick voice assistant. Think of Pulse as the fast responder who:
- Captures what you say or type instantly
- Understands what you need (treats, grooming, vet visit, etc.)
- Routes your request to Mira for smart handling

**Pulse is speed.** It gets things moving.

---

### What is Mira?
**Mira 🐕‍🦺** is the brain - your pet's personal AI concierge. Mira:
- Remembers your pet's preferences, allergies, and history
- Makes thoughtful, personalized recommendations
- Connects you with the right services (Care Concierge, Vets, Groomers)
- Never gives medical advice - routes health concerns to professionals

**Mira is intelligence.** She knows your pet deeply.

---

### How do they work together?

```
You speak/type → ⚡ PULSE captures → 🧠 MIRA reasons → ✨ Personalized response
```

**Example flow:**
1. You say: "I need treats for Mojo"
2. **Pulse** captures: Intent = order treats, Pet = Mojo
3. **Mira** reasons: Mojo likes chicken treats, is allergic to beef, prefers crunchy textures
4. **Response**: "I remember Mojo loves chicken treats! Here are some crunchy options avoiding beef..."

---

### When should I use Pulse?
Use Pulse (tap the ⚡ button) when you want to:
- Quickly order something
- Book a service
- Get fast answers
- Navigate somewhere on the platform

Pulse is perfect for action-oriented requests.

---

### When does Mira respond?
Mira responds when:
- You need personalized recommendations
- You're asking about your pet's history or preferences
- You need care guidance (not medical advice)
- You want to understand your pet's Soul Score
- Complex requests that need reasoning

---

### Does Pulse replace Mira?
**No!** They work together:
- **Pulse** = The fast capture layer (voice/text intake)
- **Mira** = The intelligence layer (memory, reasoning, personalization)

Every Pulse request is processed by Mira. Pulse just makes the interaction faster.

---

### Why two separate systems?

| Need | Solution |
|------|----------|
| Quick voice commands | Pulse handles fast |
| Deep personalization | Mira provides intelligence |
| Instant action | Pulse routes quickly |
| Pet knowledge | Mira remembers everything |

This separation allows:
1. **Speed** - Pulse responds in milliseconds
2. **Depth** - Mira provides thoughtful answers
3. **Scalability** - Each layer optimized for its purpose

## 🔒 What Mira Never Does

Mira will NEVER:
- ❌ Provide medical diagnoses
- ❌ Recommend medications or dosages
- ❌ Replace veterinary advice
- ❌ Assess symptoms medically

For ANY health concern, Mira immediately connects you with:
- Care Concierge (24/7 support)
- Trusted veterinarians
- Emergency services (if urgent)

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Use voice for speed** - Tap ⚡ and speak naturally
2. **Be specific** - "Order Mojo's favorite treats" works better than "order something"
