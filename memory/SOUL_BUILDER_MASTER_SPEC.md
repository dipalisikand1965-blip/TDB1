# PART 9: ASK MIRA vs ASK CONCIERGE

## The Golden Rule

> **Ask Mira** is the DEFAULT.
> **Ask Concierge** appears as UPGRADE when complexity is detected.

## Ask Mira (AI Layer)

- **Label:** "Ask Mira"
- **Microcopy:** "Tell me what you need for [Pet]."
- **Outputs:**
  - Tiny guidance (Learn)
  - Pick cards
  - "Let Mira do it" → creates ticket (Services)

## Ask Concierge (Human Layer)

- **Label:** "Ask Concierge"
- **Microcopy:** "We'll handle this end-to-end. WhatsApp or email."
- **Outputs:**
  - Creates/links to a ticket
  - Opens WhatsApp/email with context
  - Updates timeline through service desk
