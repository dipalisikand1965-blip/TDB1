Mira Intelligence Architecture

Mira is the AI concierge powering personalisation across all pillars. The intelligence has two layers:

1. Mira Score Engine (Claude Sonnet 4.6)

Pre-computes relevance scores for every product × pet combination, stored in mira_product_scores.

EndpointMethodPurpose
/api/mira/score-for-pet/{pet_id}POSTTrigger scoring for one pet (background)
/api/mira/batch-score-all-petsPOSTScore ALL pets in DB (background)
/api/mira/score-status/{pet_id}GETCheck scoring status + count
/api/mira/claude-picks/{pet_id}GETTop scored picks (filtered by pillar, min_score)
Auto-trigger: When a new pet opens "Mira's Picks" for the first time with no scores, the system automatically fires scoring in the background. By their second visit, full AI ranking is ready.

2. Client-Side Intelligence (getDineDims, buildMiraQuote)

Real-time personalisation using pet's doggy_soul_answers and health_data — no pre-compute needed. Works for any pet instantly.

Mira's Picks — Fetch Strategy

Fetches 5 food categories in parallel (Daily Meals, Treats & Rewards, Supplements, Frozen & Fresh, Homemade & Recipes) + services. Deduplicates by name. Enriches with Mira scores. NEVER uses a blanket pillar=dine fetch (returns breed merchandise first alphabetically).

The Mira Meal Box — NEW

6-screen personalised meal plan builder. Always shows real DB products (ranked by Mira scores). Falls back to "Mira Imagines" items if DB is empty — and persists those to products_master.
