## PRODUCTION NOTES:

**Total Duration:** ~75-80 seconds (1:15 - 1:25)

**Music:** Emotional, cinematic piano/strings. Builds from intimate to inspiring. 
Reference: "Time" by Hans Zimmer vibes, but warmer.

**Color Grading:** Warm golden tones for emotional moments, cool tones for "problem" scene, purple/pink accents for Soul/Mira moments.

**Pace:** Slow and intimate for first half, picks up energy in services montage, returns to slow for ending.

---

## SORA 2 GENERATION PLAN:

Since Sora 2 generates 4-20 second clips, we'll create:

1. **Clip 1 - Eyes Opening:** Dog's eyes, soulful gaze (4 sec)
2. **Clip 2 - The Bond:** Person and dog forehead touch (4 sec)  
3. **Clip 3 - Soul Visualization:** Abstract glowing orb with particles (4 sec)
4. **Clip 4 - Happy Moments:** Dog playing, running, joy (4 sec)
5. **Clip 5 - The Connection:** Owner hugging dog, both happy (4 sec)
6. **Clip 6 - Final Eyes:** Same dog eyes, but joyful expression (4 sec)

These can be combined with text overlays and music in post-production.
