The Founder

The person behind the platform.

In 1998, Dipali Sikand started LesConcierges® with ₹5,000 and one belief — every person deserves to be looked after properly.

That company reaches 1.6 million people across the globe. Rakesh Jhunjhunwala believed in it. The world's greatest brands trusted it.

In 2025, sitting with her dog Mystique through her final days, she applied that same belief to the one relationship most companies have never taken seriously: The bond between a person and their dog.
