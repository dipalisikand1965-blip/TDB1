CONCIERGE SYSTEM:
- Every "Book →" button → POST /api/service_desk/attach_or_create_ticket
- Body: {parent_id, pet_id, pillar, intent_primary, channel, initial_message}
- Creates ticket in service_desk_tickets collection
- WhatsApp (Meta + GupShup) inbound → webhook → ticket → Mira/Concierge → reply
