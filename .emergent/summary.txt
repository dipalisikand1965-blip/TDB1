**Key Technical Concepts**
-   **iFrame Integration Strategy**: Creating dedicated embed routes in React () that render components without the main site's layout (navbar/footer) for seamless integration into third-party sites like Shopify.
-   ** for Cross-Domain Persistence**: Using  on the client-side to store a user's email (). This allows the embedded iFrame to remember a user on their next visit and show them their personalized content (pet list) instead of the generic onboarding form.
-   **Conditional Rendering for Embeds**: Passing an  prop to components to alter their behavior, such as opening links in a new tab () to break out of the iFrame, or preventing navigation after form submission.
-   **Source Tracking**: Adding a  field to database models to differentiate data entered via the main application versus data entered through an embedded iFrame on a partner site (e.g., ).
