Soul Score System

Each pet has a "Soul Score" (0-100%) indicating how well Mira knows them:

  • 0-25%: Basic info only - name, breed
  • 26-50%: Personality emerging - temperament, energy
  • 51-75%: Deep knowledge - allergies, preferences, social behavior
  • 76-100%: Complete soul - family, relationships, full history

PICKS: Personalized Product Recommendations NEW

90-100% Breed-Specific Personalization

Products are automatically matched to each pet's breed, showing "Made with love for [Pet Name]"

How PICKS Works

  1. Breed Detection: System reads pet's breed from their profile
  2. Product Matching: Filters products designed for that breed
  3. Pillar Context: Only shows products relevant to current pillar (e.g., cakes on /celebrate)
  4. Beautiful Display: Icon cards for breed PICKS, real images for Shopify products

Rainbow Bridge Feature NEW

Handling pet loss with compassion and dignity.

How It Works

  1. Pet parent marks their pet as "crossed the rainbow bridge"
  2. Pet record is preserved (never deleted)
  3. UI shows gentle memorial indicators
  4. Mira AI becomes aware and responds with compassion
  5. No product suggestions - only emotional support

Mira's Response

When asked about a pet that has passed:

"I'm so sorry for your loss. Mystique crossed the rainbow bridge on March 7, 2026. She was such a calm, golden little Shih Tzu who loved liver treats and cheese... If you'd like, we can talk about her, write a tribute together, or think of a way to honor her memory."
