✅ Current approach: Blank product templates + breed illustration picker. Customer picks which Yappy illustration they want, adds to cart with illustration metadata, Concierge fulfils the order.

FlatArtPickerCard Flow

  1. Customer opens Soul Made category in any ContentModal (Dine/Go/Play/Celebrate)
  2. Sees toggle: 🎨 Watercolour | 🐾 Flat Art
  3. Clicks "🐾 Flat Art" → grid switches to FlatArtPickerCard components
  4. Each card shows: blank product template image + row of breed illustration thumbnails
  5. Customer taps an illustration (e.g., "Ginger") → gold border appears on selected
  6. Price shown: ₹299–₹999 (from FLAT_ART_PRICES map in FlatArtPickerCard.jsx)
  7. Clicks "Add to Cart — Ginger" → cart item added with customDetails.illustration_url
  8. CartSidebar shows illustration thumbnail + "🐾 Flat Art · Ginger · Indie · For Mojo"

ContentModal soul_made fetch pattern

// Fetches 3 things in parallel for soul_made category:
const [r1, r2, r3] = await Promise.all([
  fetch(`/api/mockups/breed-products?breed=${breed}&pillar={pillar}&limit=60`), // watercolour
