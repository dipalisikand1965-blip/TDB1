12. AI Product Description Enhancer

Uses Emergent LLM (GPT-4) to automatically rewrite product descriptions for better engagement and Mira AI understanding.

🤖 What It Does

Input (Original Description)

"Dog cake with chicken flavor. 500g."

Output (AI Enhanced)

"Treat your furry friend to this delicious chicken-flavored birthday cake! 🎂 Made with love for your pet's special day, this 500g celebration cake is perfect for making memories."

📋 Features

  • Batch Processing: Enhances up to 20 products at a time
  • Warm Tone: Speaks to pet parents with professional yet friendly language
  • Emoji Integration: Adds 1-2 relevant emojis for engagement
  • Mira-Optimized: Descriptions help Mira AI recommend products better
  • Database Update: Saves enhanced descriptions to ai_description field

⚙️ API Endpoints

EndpointMethodPurpose
/api/admin/products/enhance-descriptionsPOSTEnhance all products (batch)
/api/admin/products/enhance-single/{id}POSTEnhance single product
⚠️ Note: Batch enhancement takes 2-5 minutes as it processes products one by one to respect API rate limits.
